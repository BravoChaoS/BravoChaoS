Research Interests

My research interests broadly lie in confidential computing and privacy preservation. In recent years, I have specifically focused on the following areas:

  • Confidential distributed system security (e.g., blockchain, trusted execution environments (TEE)).
  • AI-related security (e.g., training and inference security, data privacy).
